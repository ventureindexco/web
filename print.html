Venture Index

Introduction

Venture Index is an attempt to index the Philippines local startup ecosystem.

Our goal is to index the following:

  • Communities
  • Funding
  • Services
  • Resources
  • Events (upcoming & ongoing)
  • Recruitment (hiring & for hire)

Contribute

Your ideas, feedback, and suggestions are welcome.

Contribute thru Google Forms

  • You may send us your feedback at our Google Forms Feedback Form

Contribute thru Telegram

  • You may send us your feedback at our Telegram Group.

Contribute thru GitHub

  • This website's entire source code is publicly available on our GitHub Repository. We use the Markdown syntax to write our pages.
  • To suggest an edit on specific pages, just click the top-right edit button of that page. Submitting your proposed changes will create a "pull-request" which we will review.
  • Once approved by us, your proposed changes will be immediately reflected in our website. You may check our repository's GitHub Actions tab to view the latest build progress and status.
  • You may also create issues at our repository's GitHub Issues tab. Thank you.
